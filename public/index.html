About Global Max Services

The Backbone of American Freight

Global Max Services LLC is a federally authorized interstate property carrier headquartered in Lombard, Illinois, just outside Chicago. Since our founding, we've built a reputation for consistent performance, regulatory compliance, and a safety-first approach to every load we haul.

We operate a modern fleet of 99 power units staffed by 115 professionally trained drivers who collectively log over 15 million miles annually across the continental United States. Our drivers aren't just behind the wheel — they're the face of our company, rigorously trained in compliance, defensive driving, and cargo handling.

Our operating authority covers general freight, motor vehicle transport, and the movement of oversized machinery and large objects. That range of authorization, combined with our scale and safety record, makes us a versatile and dependable partner for shippers who need more than just capacity — they need a carrier they can trust.

Our Services

What We Move

Global Max is authorized by the FMCSA to transport a wide range of cargo types. From dry goods and retail inventory to automobiles and industrial equipment, we provide the capacity, equipment, and expertise to move your freight safely and on schedule.

General Freight

Full truckload and partial capacity for palletized goods, retail inventory, manufacturing supplies, and commercial shipments moving coast-to-coast. We handle the scheduling, documentation, and delivery confirmation so your supply chain keeps running without interruptions.

Motor Vehicle Transport

Licensed and insured for the transportation of automobiles, trucks, and fleet vehicles. We work with dealerships, auction houses, manufacturers, and private shippers to move vehicles safely across state lines with full insurance coverage and real-time tracking throughout transit.

Machinery & Oversize Loads

Heavy equipment, industrial machinery, construction gear, and other large objects that require specialized handling, route planning, and permit coordination. Our team manages the logistics from loading to delivery, including pilot cars and escort services when required.

Dedicated Contract Routes

For shippers who need guaranteed capacity on their most critical lanes, we offer dedicated contract hauling with assigned equipment and drivers. You get consistent pickup and delivery windows, a single point of contact, and priority scheduling that takes the uncertainty out of your logistics.

End-to-End Logistics Coordination

From the moment a load is dispatched to the final delivery confirmation, our operations team provides full visibility into every shipment. You get proactive communication, detailed documentation, BOL management, and a responsive dispatch team that answers the phone when it matters most.

Expedited & Time-Critical Freight

When delivery windows are tight and there's no room for delays, our expedited service puts your freight on dedicated equipment with team drivers and priority routing. We coordinate every leg of the transit to meet your most urgent deadlines — including nights, weekends, and holidays.
