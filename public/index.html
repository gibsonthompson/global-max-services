Who We Are

The Backbone of American Freight

Global Max Services LLC is a federally authorized interstate property carrier headquartered in Lombard, Illinois. We operate a modern fleet of 99 power units staffed by 115 professional drivers, logging over 15 million miles annually across the continental United States.

Our authority covers general freight, motor vehicle transport, and oversized machinery and large object hauling — making us a versatile partner for shippers who need reliability at scale, not just capacity.
